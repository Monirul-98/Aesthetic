Who?

When you work with our team of full-service planners and designers, you’re covered. From your initial vision, to venue and vendor selection, to budget and timeline development, to design execution and more, we handle it all.We are committed to finding you the perfect team for your day.

 

What?

We offer full and partial planning and design, as well as month-of coordination services and consulting. Each of our events is different since every client is unique. Therefore, after we learn about the scope of your event, along with your needs and wishes, we will develop a completely custom package tailored exclusively to suit your personality and desires.

 

Where?

We have office in Chittagong. We plan and design events all over Chittagong. We're also willing and able to travel to Europe, the Caribbean, and anywhere else worldwide - wherever you want to celebrate!
